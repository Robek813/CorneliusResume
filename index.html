Lord Baron Cornelius Cornwellingtonsworth IV

Lord Baron of Cornwellingtonshire and surrounding isles

The following paragraph will serve as a descriptive biography of Lord Baron Cornelius Cornwellingtonsworth IV. From a young age Lord Cornelius was an impish and fickle cuss that only liked to eat caviar and buttercream-frosted Turkish Delights. His demeanor and temperment were so insufferable that when he was just 5 years old his own mother decided that he should undergo education early and sent for the most accomplished Dog trainer in all of Cornwellingtonshire. After Lord Cornelius' thorough canine behavioral conditioning was complete, he became much more agreeable and obedient to his parents. However, he was caught red-handed conducting himself very unlike the stately position that his famliy name would suggest. He was found drinking from toliet bowls, chewing on scraps of food from the garbage and licking himself clean instead of taking a bath. Later, after having the easiest life of any human or dog, Lord Cornelius excepted his Baron title and became the most mediocre ruler Cornwellingtonshire had ever endured, and to this day is celebrated each year on Cornwellingtonsworth's day as the most average and uninspiring Baron ever to bless the British isles.
